Personal Biography
==================

I am a software engineer driven to build tools with a purpose, whether that means helping others achieve their goals or
creating a business.

As a kid, I loved building with Meccano. But the first time I saw a mouse pointer move across a screen, everything
changed. That simple magic hooked me on programming since then.

I have been writing code since that time when I was twelve. By my early twenties, I turned that passion into a career,
freelancing to build custom e-commerce sites and digital tools for clients.

30 years later, I pour my energy into three main
passions: [Financial Literacy](https://www.financialbreaker.com), `Software Engineering`, and
[Electronic Music Production](https://open.spotify.com/artist/42xGjvlq05ZQ90f398AhGh?si=zeroAFZKST-uKi-R7PqMTQ).

> Give me a purpose and some time, and I will build the solution.

# The Mandalorian Creed: Learning by Doing was Always the Way

It comes from my instinct to face challenges head-on and figure them out. It is not just about writing code or
studying music theory. It is about mastering the craft by understanding.

> I hear and I forget. I see and I remember. I do and I understand.
>
> -- Confucio

This path, driven by an unbeatable instinct of curiosity, comes with its own consequences. Pure theory approaches always
bring me a kind of anxiety about losing time. I need to get my hands dirty and get scars on my skin to figure out by
myself how things really work, uncovering the lessons that reading books or getting certifications just don't expose.
